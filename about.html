Who We Are

Sunastro Limited

Sunastro Limited is a registered and dynamic Kenyan company headquartered in Nairobi, specializing in premium custom furniture solutions, general supplies, construction services, and renewable energy feedstock. Our operations blend creativity, reliability, and sustainability across sectors to meet the evolving needs of modern corporate and institutional clients.

Since our establishment, we've grown to become a trusted partner for businesses and institutions across East Africa, delivering excellence through innovative solutions and sustainable practices.

Contact Us

Our Vision

To be a leading multi-sector solutions provider in East Africa, delivering excellence in design, construction, supply chain, and sustainable energy for a better future.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and environmentally responsible products and services across furniture, general supplies, construction, and renewable energy—empowering businesses and communities through quality and sustainability.

Our Services

Custom Furniture

High-end, KILN-dried wood furniture tailored to client specifications with in-house design consultancy for space optimization.

  • 10-year guarantee on manufacturing defects
  • Extensive portfolio from our concept catalogue
  • Office and institutional furniture solutions

General Supplies

Comprehensive supply solutions for businesses and institutions across Kenya.

  • Office equipment and consumables
  • Sanitary and hygiene products
  • ICT hardware and accessories
  • Countrywide delivery and tender compliance

Construction Services

NCA registered construction services for diverse building needs.

  • Registered under NCA Category 6 (Building & Road Works)
  • Civil works, renovations, and infrastructure projects
  • Skilled technical team and efficient project supervision

Renewable Energy

Supporting Kenya's clean energy transition through sustainable solutions.

  • Organic/industrial waste-based feedstock for SAF
  • Key supplier to Mobimart Systems Limited
  • Supporting circular economy initiatives
