About Us

Sunastro Limited

Sunastro Limited is a registered and dynamic Kenyan company headquartered in Nairobi, specializing in the delivery of premium custom furniture solutions, general supplies, construction services, and renewable energy feedstock. Our operations blend creativity, reliability, and sustainability across sectors to meet the evolving needs of modern corporate and institutional clients.

Vision: To be a leading multi-sector solutions provider in East Africa, delivering excellence in design, construction, supply chain, and sustainable energy for a better future.

Mission: To provide innovative, reliable, and environmentally responsible products and services across furniture, general supplies, construction, and renewable energy—empowering businesses and communities through quality and sustainability.
